Development Process

A clear, structured process from idea to production-ready design.

1
Requirement Study & Feasibility
Clarify technical and business requirements, standards, and constraints.
2
System & Architecture Design
Choose MCU/Soc, interfaces, power, protection and software architecture.
3
Hardware Design
Schematics, PCB layout, BOM, EMC/industrial design considerations.
4
Firmware & Software
RTOS/bare-metal or Embedded Linux, bootloaders, protocols, diagnostics.
5
Prototype & Testing
Bring-up, functional verification, field testing, iterations.
6
Certification Support
Assist with design aspects for CE/UL/other regulatory needs where required.
7
Production Handover
Provide schematics, PCB, BOM, Gerber, firmware, test procedures and documentation.
